LinkHub Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LinkHub. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how your data is protected when you use our chat service.

By using LinkHub, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Username / display name
  • Optional avatar or emoji
  • Messages you send in the chat

B. Automatically Collected Information

Through Firebase, we may collect:

  • Device type (Android, browser)
  • Connection information
  • Message timestamps

3. How Your Data Is Used

  • Display chat messages in real time
  • Keep the chat functional and secure
  • Prevent abuse or spam
  • Improve app performance

We do not sell, trade, or share data with advertisers.

4. Data Storage

LinkHub uses Firebase Realtime Database to store chat messages. Messages remain in the database until deleted by an administrator. Firebase applies its own security protections.

5. Cookies & Tracking

LinkHub does not use cookies. Firebase may collect basic analytics for performance and security.

6. Third-Party Services

LinkHub uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase – Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages – Site Policy

7. Data Retention

Messages may remain in the database until manually removed. If you request message removal, we will delete it within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Children’s Privacy

LinkHub is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Security

We use Firebase’s built-in security rules to protect your data, including encrypted communication and controlled read/write permissions.

10. Your Choices

  • Use any username you choose
  • Delete your messages (if enabled)
  • Request message deletion by contacting us

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
